Workout Beat Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

English

Workout Beat is a workout rhythm and metronome app for iPhone. This app is designed to work primarily on-device and does not require account registration or sign-in.

1. Information we collect

  • We do not intentionally collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, health data, payment data, or contacts.
  • We do not provide in-app sign-in, membership, or cloud upload features in the current version.
  • Your app preferences, such as tempo, workout duration, sound options, and light settings, may be stored locally on your device so the app can remember your setup.

2. Camera permission

If you enable flash sync, the app may request camera permission only to access the iPhone flashlight and pulse it with the beat. The app does not use this permission to take photos, record video, or upload imagery.

3. Background audio

The app can continue audio playback while running in the background so your workout rhythm can continue during exercise.

4. Sharing and analytics

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs in the current version.
  • We do not use account-based analytics or cloud profile tracking in the current version.

5. Changes

If the app adds new data features in the future, this policy will be updated on this page.

6. Support

Support information is available on the support page.
